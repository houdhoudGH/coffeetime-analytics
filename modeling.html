Conclusion & Recommendation

SARIMAX is the Recommended Model

Based on our analysis, SARIMAX outperforms other models with the lowest MAE of 3.54. It successfully captures the weekly seasonality in coffee sales (higher sales on weekdays, lower on weekends) and can be used for:

  • Inventory planning and stock management
  • Staff scheduling based on predicted demand
  • Revenue forecasting and budgeting
  • Identifying peak hours and optimizing operations
