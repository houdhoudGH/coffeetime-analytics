Frequently Asked Questions

What data is used?

We use coffee shop sales data from 2024, including transaction amounts, times, and coffee types.

What models are used?

We compare ARIMA, Auto ARIMA, and SARIMAX for time series forecasting.

Can I download the data?

The raw CSV data is available in the /data/raw directory of this project.

How accurate are forecasts?

SARIMAX achieves the best MAE of 3.54, making it suitable for short-term predictions.
